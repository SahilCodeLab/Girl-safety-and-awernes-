Recognizing Signs of Abuse

Unexplained Injuries: Agar koi ladki ya aurat kisi bhi tareeke ki chhot ka shikar hoti hai, chahe wo jismani ho ya jazbati, to use kabhi bhi chupana nahi chahiye. Agar aapke kareeb kisi ne aapko nuqsan pohanchaya ho, chahe wo jismani ho ya jazbati, toh apne maa-baap ya trusted doston ke saath zaroor share karein. Kisi bhi tarah ka abuse, chahe wo jazbati ho ya jismani, bardasht nahi karna chahiye. Yeh samajhna zaroori hai ki apne dard ko chupana sirf aapki sehat aur izzat ko nuqsan pohanchayega. Aapko hamesha surakshit aur support milna chahiye. Kabhi bhi madad lene aur apne dukh ko zindagi mein share karne se na daren.

Withdrawal from Friends: Agar koi ladki achanak apne doston ya family se door ho jaati hai ya apni normal routine se apne aapko alag kar leti hai, to yeh ek warning sign ho sakta hai. Aise behavior changes is baat ki taraf ishara karte hain ke kuch gehra masla uske andar chal raha hai. Agar aapko yeh dekhe, toh us ladki se baat karein aur usse yeh ehsaas dilayein ke wo kabhi bhi apne dard ko share kar sakti hai, aur wo akeli nahi hai.

Fear of Certain Individuals: Agar koi ladki ya aurat kisi khaas shakhs ke aas paas ghabraahat ya dar mehsoos karti hai, toh is baat ko gambhirta se lena zaroori hai. Dar ki ehsaas emotional ya physical abuse ka nishan ho sakta hai. Yeh zaroori hai ki is baat ko samjha jaye aur us ladki ko support diya jaye, taaki wo apni safety aur well-being ke liye awaaz utha sake.

Naked is not freedom

1.Understand the two images: Is image mein jo do lolipops dikhaye gaye hain, unse ek bohot zaroori baat samjhayi gayi hai. Pehla lolipop ek cover mein hai, jo 'with hijab' ko dikhata hai – jaise ek ladki jo hijab pehenti hai, apni izzat aur modesty ka dhyan rakhti hai. Dusra lolipop bina cover ke hai, jo 'without hijab' ko symbol karta hai, aur iske aas paas keede hain, jo yeh dikhate hain ki jab aap apne aap ko cover nahi karti, toh aap unwanted attention ko attract karti hain, jo aapke liye dangerous ho sakta hai.

2.It is for all religions: Yeh baat sabhi dharmon ke liye hai, na ki sirf kisi ek dharm ke liye. Har dharm mein izzat aur modesty ko samajhne ka apna tareeqa hai. Hum yeh nahi keh rahe hain ki aapko hijab pehna chahiye ya kisi specific dharm ka palan karna chahiye. Hum sirf yeh keh rahe hain ki har ladki ko apne sharir ko izzat deni chahiye, aur apni izzat aur safety ke liye decent aur modest kapde pehene chahiye.

3.The scarf protects you: "Chahe aap jeans aur top pehen rahi hon, suit pehene hon ya koi aur kapda, zaroori hai ke apne jism ko aise dhak kar rakhein ke aapka body shape zyada numayan na ho. Islam mein apne jism ko is tarah zahir karna gunaah mana gaya hai, jo sirf aapko nahi, balke dekhne walon ko bhi galat soch aur gunahon mein mubtala kar sakta hai. Dupatta ya kisi aur layer se apne aap ko dhak kar aap izzat aur hifazat mehsoos kar sakti hain aur gunaahon se door reh sakti hain."

Love trap

You Are Not a Product
Agar koi jo aapko pehle chhod chuka tha, wapis aake apni mohabbat ka izhaar kare, toh yeh ek red flag hai. Dhyan rakhein—yeh asal mohabbat nahi hai; yeh is baat ka ishaara hai ke woh aapko phir se apne faayde ke liye istemal karna chahta hai. Yaad rakhein, aap koi product nahi ho jo har koi apne hisaab se istemal kare. Apne dil ki hifazat karein aur kabhi bhi kisi ko apni keemat tay karne na dein. Aapki izzat ki koi keemat nahi hoti, aur aap aise mohabbat ke laayak hain jo aapko upar uthaye, na ke neeche giraaye.

Pleasure vs. Regret
Ladkiyon, shaadi se pehle kisi bhi tarah ki physical relationship se bachna bohot zaroori hai. Aaj ka sukoon kal ke sharm aur pareshani mein badal sakta hai. Yad rakhein, aapki izzat aapke liye bohot qeemti hai, aur uska khayal rakhna aapka haq hai.

Understanding and Effort
Kisi bhi rishte mein dono taraf se jazbaat aur mehnat honi chahiye. Agar sirf ek insaan emotional investment kare, toh rishte mein imbalance aur disappointment ho sakta hai. Rishte mein apni izzat aur jazbaat ko barqarar rakhein aur apne saath dishonest logon se bachke rahiye.

Recognizing Exploitation
Bohot si ladkiyan apne jazbaat mein behke, pyaar ke chaakar mein aise logon ke shikaar ho jaati hain jo sirf unhe apne faayde ke liye istemal karte hain. Agar koi aapko jaldi se pyaar dikhata hai, toh uski niyat par shaq karna zaroori hai.

Maintaining Awareness
Rishte mein hamesha satark rehna zaroori hai. Apni feelings ki hifazat karne ke liye, apne boundaries set karein, aur kisiko blind trust dene se pehle unki sacchai jaanch lein. Apne jazbaat ko samajhne ke liye waqt nikaalein aur apne rishte ko achhe se samjhein.

Tips for Traps
- Kisi bhi insaan ko apne dil mein jagah dene se pehle, uske baare mein achhe se jaanch kar lein.
- Unke actions ko dekhein, jo unke shabdon se milte ho. Asli log apni commitment ko waqt ke saath dikhate hain.
- Apne doston se madad lein jo aapko rishte ke baare mein achhi raaye de sakte hain aur unka perspective aapko mil sakta hai.

Safety Rules

1. Always be aware of your surroundings: Stay alert and recognize the people around you. Pay attention to your instincts.

2. Avoid sharing personal information: Don’t share your home address or personal details with strangers, especially online.

3. Trust your instincts: If something feels wrong, don’t hesitate to seek help or move to a safer location.

4. Stay connected: Keep your mobile phone charged and easily accessible. Share your location with a trusted friend or family member.

POSCO Act

POSCO Act, 2012, primarily focuses on protecting children from sexual offenses. While its main emphasis is on children, women can be involved in the context of being guardians or caregivers. The Act ensures that adults responsible for children, including women, have a role in reporting and preventing abuse. However, it does not directly address offenses against women; separate laws exist for that, such as the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act.

Punishments under the POSCO Act

1. Sexual Assault: Punishment for sexual assault can range from 3 to 5 years of imprisonment, which may extend to life imprisonment depending on the severity of the crime.

2. Child Pornography: Anyone caught engaging in child pornography may face rigorous imprisonment for a minimum of 5 years, extending to 7 years.

3. Aggravated Sexual Assault: This is treated with utmost seriousness, resulting in imprisonment of 10 years to life, along with fines.

4. Under the POCSO Act : if a case arises in West Bengal, there is a possibility of the death penalty, but this applies only in specific circumstances. The Act provides for severe penalties for certain types of sexual assault or harassment against minors. However, the imposition of such a penalty depends on the investigation and the evidence presented in each case.

Self-Defense (During Periods)

1. Embrace Physical Sensitivity:

Understand and accept the changes your body goes through during your period. Engage in gentle exercises like yoga or stretching to enhance your physical awareness and flexibility. These activities not only strengthen your body but also help you feel more in control. When you are physically in tune with yourself, it boosts your confidence in defending yourself if necessary.

1.Cultivate a Positive Mindset:

common to feel vulnerable during your period, but you can combat negative thoughts by using positive affirmations. Remind yourself, “I am strong, I am capable, and I can protect myself.” This practice fosters emotional resilience and empowers you to face any challenging situation with a strong mindset. When you believe in your strength, you’re more likely to respond assertively and confidently in any circumstance.

Self-Defense Tips

1. Take Self-Defense Classes: Empower yourself with self-defense training to better protect yourself.

2. Carry a Personal Alarm: A loud alarm can draw attention and deter potential attackers.

3. Use Everyday Items: Items like keys, pens, or any small object can be used for self-defense.

4. Know Escape Routes: Be aware of your surroundings and identify escape routes when walking alone.
